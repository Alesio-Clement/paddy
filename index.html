About me

I'm Patrick Wakasiaka, a Senior Product Manager and UI/UX Designer from Nairobi, Kenya, specializing in web design and digital product design. I thrive on transforming complex challenges into seamless, intuitive, and visually compelling user experiences.

As a Senior Product Manager, I bridge the gap between design, development, and business strategy, ensuring that every product I work on is not only functional and user-friendly but also aligned with business goals. My expertise in UI/UX design allows me to craft products that are both aesthetically appealing and highly effective.

With experience designing for renowned brands, I bring a creative and strategic approach to every project, ensuring that your brand identity and message stand out in the most impactful way.
